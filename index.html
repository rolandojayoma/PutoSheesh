Seje's Puto Cheese Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (120g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 1 cup (150g) grated cheese (e.g., cheddar or Edam)
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) evaporated milk
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) water
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 cup (60g) butter, melted
  • 1 egg
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your steamer. Grease the molds lightly with butter or oil to prevent sticking.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, grated cheese, baking powder, and salt. Mix well.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together evaporated milk, water, melted butter, and egg.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients. Mix until well combined and smooth.
  5. Fill each mold with the batter until about 3/4 full.
  6. Place the filled molds in the steamer. Steam for about 10-15 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Once cooked, remove from the steamer and let cool for a few minutes.
  8. Serve warm and enjoy your delicious Puto Cheese!
